# Axe Bass — Adventure Time Prop

A full-scale replica of the Axe Bass from Adventure Time, modelled from reference and designed to be assembled in interlocking sections.

## Overview
Over the 2025 inter-semester break, a family member had requested me to re-create Marceline's axe bass from Adventure Time. As I've never done a full project from conception to fully constructed AND painted, I thought it was a good challenge to do over the course of a week.

## How it works
# Key design decisions
-- I wanted to get the proportions identical to the show, down to the smallest details
-- Small functionality like being able to use a bass strap, the ability to turn the knobs and pegs
-- Some realism, the original bass had a straight head, but I opted to slant it and carved channels for the actual string to go through
-- Because of the size, the body and neck had to be split into smaller pieces to fit on my 245x245 printer plate
# How the parts fit together
-- Very simple keying of parts. The body, neck, and tail spike all need to be assembled, and the key makes orienting them really easy; they fit snugly together
-- Used black painted toothpicks for the frets, and a white fishing wire for the strings
-- Used Loctite 401 to lock the parts on top of the already tight keys
